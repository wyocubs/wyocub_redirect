Wyomissing Cub Scouts Pack 413

Are you looking for an exciting, enriching experience for your son? Wyomissing Cub Scouts is the perfect place for boys from kindergarten to 5th grade to embark on adventures, learn new skills, and make lifelong friends.

Our Cub Scouts enjoy two camping weekends each year, including an unforgettable trip to Hawk Mountain, where they can try archery, fishing, zip lines, and other exciting activities. Adventure is always around the corner!

From designing and launching model rockets to building and racing boats in our Rain-gutter Regatta, your son will gain hands-on experience in a variety of skills. Our Pinewood Derby race also challenges the boys to design and build their own cars for a thrilling competition.

Being a Cub Scout means being part of a close-knit group of friends. Whether they're hiking in nature, bowling, or enjoying a pool party at the Wyomissing Pool, our scouts bond through fun and engaging activities. Your son will develop social skills and make new friends that can last a lifetime.

Cub Scouts participate in community service projects, including cleaning up the neighborhood on Earth Day and walking in the Memorial Day parade. These activities teach the value of responsibility, empathy, and giving back to the community.

Our program is packed with fun activities and games. From hiking in nature to enjoying a pool party, there's always something exciting happening. Your son will have a blast while learning and growing in a supportive environment.
